About us

About us

Our mission is to be the most sustainable restaurant in Sofia by sourcing our ingredients from local farms only, supplementing produce with herbs grown in our own garden, and giving back to the community through serving only bio and fresh vegetarian and vegan foods.

V for Vegetarian is not your mainstream restaurant. It's a place, where you will feel cozy, warm and at home. By providing our visitors with refreshing drinks and hearty foods every day, we aim to inspire people to be kind to each other and be a better version of themselves.

Celebrate life's special moments, celebrate friends, celebrate family. Choose us.
